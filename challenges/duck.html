Right click in the browser window and click the last option, "Inspect".

Open the "Elements" tab and expand the body section in the HTML.

Keep expanding down the HTML tree until you find the <a> tag. There, you will find an invisible class.

Double click the class and press the delete key to remove the class. The button will now be visible to click.
