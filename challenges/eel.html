Right click in the browser window and click the last option, "Inspect".

Open the "Elements" tab and expand the body section in the HTML.

Keep expanding down the HTML tree until you find the <a> tag. There, you will find the href attribute.

Right click on the href and click edit attribute. Update it to the animal you see in the data-next-animal attribute. The <a> will now link to the correct page.
