Right click in the browser window and click the last option, "Inspect".

Open the "Elements" tab and expand the body section in the HTML.

Keep expanding down the HTML tree until you find the password-img class.

On the right side of the inspector, you will see a Styles section. After clicking the element, you will see its current styles.

Uncheck the visibility: hidden style to show the image containing our password.
